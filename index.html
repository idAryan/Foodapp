About

Hey I am Aryan. Our idea is to build application integrated with machine learning models that is able to detect the daily basis food item and automatically fetch the data from the database and calculate the calories vitamins and nutritional consumtion to help the people to keep track there goals.

We care about your health

It is important to take care of your health track your daily diet consumption using our application, our app will detect the image clicked by your camera and on selecting the quantity of consumption you will store the daily calrie vitamin consumtion.

Image Recognition

Camera Integration: Users can take pictures of their meals. Image Processing: The app uses machine learning to recognize different food items in the image

Nutritional Database

Food Database: A comprehensive database containing nutritional information for a wide variety of foods. Integration: The app matches recognized foods with entries in the nutritional database.

Daily Tracking

Food Log: Users can log their daily food intake. Nutritional Summary: Provide a summary of daily calorie and vitamin consumption. Historical Data: Users can view their nutritional intake over time.
